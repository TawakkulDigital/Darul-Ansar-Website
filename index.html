Introduction

Darul Ansar is an organization dedicated to supporting New Muslim converts through education and community engagement. Our mission is to empower new Muslims by fostering a sense of belonging and providing accessible Islamic knowledge. In alignment with this vision, we offer courses, webinars, and in-person events tailored specifically to the needs of new Muslims.

The Darul Ansar Team

Moulana Uzair Mutaqi

Moulana Uzair completed his Quran memorization in 2015 at Iqra Darul Ehsan, NY, and earned his Alimiyyah degree from the Islamic Institute of Atlanta in 2023. He is the Co-Founder of Darul Ansar, an initiative dedicated to supporting new Muslims. Mawlana Uzair also serves as an instructor at the Islamic Institute of Atlanta, where he teaches traditional Islamic sciences and frequently engages youth through events and lectures. Additionally, he teaches at Al-Qasim Institute’s weekend Islamic studies program. He holds an Ijazah in the Qira’ah of Hafs, is pursuing advanced studies in Fiqh, and is currently enrolled in a Master’s program in Islamic Finance at INCEIF University.

Hafiz Ammar Mutaqi

Hafiz Ammar is a dedicated student of Islamic sciences with a strong commitment to education and community service. He completed his Quran memorization (Hifdh) at the Islamic Institute of Atlanta in 2020 and is currently advancing his studies in the Alimiyyah Program at the same institute. He is pursuing an Ijazah in the Qira’ah of Hafs under Shaykh Mustafa Nabawi. As Co-Founder of Darul Ansar, Hafiz Ammar oversees marketing and administration, working to grow the organization’s outreach. Passionate about youth mentorship, he actively engages young Muslims across Metro Atlanta, fostering their spiritual and personal development.

Alimah Umm Uzair

Born and raised in Karachi, Pakistan, Alimah Umm Uzair belongs to the pioneer batch of Zainab Center, where she began her journey of traditional Islamic learning in 2003. She studied Tajweed, Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, Usul al-Fiqh, and Arabic Grammar under qualified scholars and received her Fadila degree in 2015. She is the Co-Founder of Darul Ansar and has successfully managed a family business for over seven years. In addition to her business leadership, she continues to teach Tajweed, Tafseer, and Fiqh at Zainab Center, inspiring students with her depth of knowledge and dedication.

Alimah Bint Asif

Born and raised in Danbury, Connecticut, Alimah Bint Asif began her Islamic education early, enrolling in the Alimiyyah program at Darul Uloom Al Madania in Buffalo, NY. After six years of intensive study in Hadith, Tafsir, Aqidah, and Fiqh, she graduated in 2020. She has since taught Quran/Tajweed, Fiqh, History, and Hadith at Danbury Masjid ISWC both online and in person, and currently serves as an Instructor at Darul Ansar. In 2024, she earned her BBA in Business Management from Western Connecticut State University and is now pursuing an Ijaaza through Darul Qasim’s Quran program.
